How to Use the To-Do List

Step 1. Add an entry in the textbox below, and press the "Add" button.

Step 2. Once the entry is complete, you can cross it out by clicking on the entry.

Step 3. To remove an entry, double click on the entry to remove it entirely.

Step 4. To remove every entry on the list, press the "Clear" button.
